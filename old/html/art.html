Celebrity PAINTING
January, 2017
82" x 41" acrylic on canvas; the size of the painting/form of application makes any human form difficult to distinguish when seen in person, so the celebrity figures can only be seen when the painting is viewed through a screen (cell phone, camera, etc.). This work has been acquired by Dean Thomas Dunne.
